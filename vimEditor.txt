Commands for Vim Editor:
    vi -> open vim editor.
        ex: vi file.txt

    i -> enter into insert mode.
        ex: i

    esc -> edit mode.
        ex: esc

    ZZ -> save & quit.
        ex: esc then press ZZ

    :q! -> exit without save.
        ex: esc then press :q!

    :w -> save without exit.
        ex: esc then :w

    :wq -> save & exit
        ex: esc then :wq
